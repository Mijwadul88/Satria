About Satria Berkah Kencana

Our Mission

Satria Berkah Kencana is dedicated to providing high-quality coconut-based products that empower agricultural practices worldwide. We focus on sustainability, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in coconut commodity trading, known for our premium products and commitment to sustainable agricultural practices.

Our Premium Products

Cocopeat

An excellent growing medium for hydroponics and soil mixtures. Our cocopeat retains moisture and provides ideal aeration for plant roots.

  • Moisture: 15-20%
  • pH: 5.5-6.5
  • EC: 0.5-1.0 mS/cm

Cocofiber

A natural fiber extracted from coconut husks. Ideal for erosion control, mattresses, upholstery, and horticultural applications.

  • Length: 5-30 cm
  • Moisture: 12-15%
  • Impurity: < 5%

Coconut Briquettes

High-quality briquettes made from coconut shells, perfect for cooking, heating, and industrial applications.

  • Fixed Carbon: 75-85%
  • Ash: 2-4%
  • Volatile Matter: 10-15%
